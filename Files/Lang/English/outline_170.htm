Shift Indicator                                                                                             Object

 

The SHIFT INDICATOR object is an indicator that can appear when the Graffiti area for input is in upper-case mode. Otherwise, the shift indicator does not appear on the form. If you desire a shift indicator, you must place this object on your display form at design time.
